FELLOWSHIPS
  1. Membership
  2. Distinguished Fellowship

The FWIGS is the Institute's honor award/recognition reserved for accomplished women executives who have not only achieved distinctions in their careers and demonstrated prolific innovation in business practice. Only very few in this apex category are offered annually to deserving personalities of outstanding distinctions. What all Fellows share, regardless of their professional background or nationality, is a desire to make meaningful positive change in the world in collaboration with peers across sectors and borders. Successful candidates are eager to leverage the global network of WIGS Fellowships and engage, over their lifetime as FWIGS (fellows of Win Institute for Gender Studies), in constructive projects and initiatives designed to enhance the values of WIGS Fellowships: integrity, greatness, peace, prosperity, and justice.
