About Us

Empowering African Women: Win Institute for Gender Studies

Win Institute for Gender Studies (WIGS) is the premier learning community in the region that empowers women with cutting-edge leadership and gender management skills. Our focus is on reshaping the African narrative by positioning women in governance, business, and education to drive regional and national development. Our rigorous curriculum, supported by practical case studies, is designed to unlock the full potential of women in leadership and management roles.
